Abhyas

Abhyas – The Learning Enhancement System (LES)

Abhyas is a Learning Enhancement System (LES) designed to support schools and teachers with structured assessments, in-depth performance tracking, and streamlined intervention strategies.

Built to complement classroom instruction, Abhyas ensures that learning modules align with the curriculum while providing deep performance insights. Instead of relying on manual tracking and guesswork, teachers can download practice test reports that highlight topic-wise, subject-wise, and subtopic-wise student performance, saving valuable time and enabling precise interventions.

For school management, Abhyas offers real-time oversight of student progress, teacher effectiveness, and curriculum impact, allowing data-backed academic and operational decisions.

Key Features:
  • Classroom Performance Insights - Provides real-time data on student strengths and weaknesses for informed teaching.
  • Actionable Data Reports – Auto-generates subject-wise and topic-wise analysis, eliminating manual tracking.
  • Validated Assessments – The Assess – Practice – Test cycle reinforces knowledge retention and exam readiness.
  • Minimal Time, Maximum Impact – Requires only 30-40 minutes per day for measurable student improvement.
  • Competitive Exam Readiness – Equips students for K-12, JEE, NEET, and global standardized tests.
  • Seamless School Integration – Customizable for schools and coaching centers, aligning with their curriculum.

DigiVal

DigiVal – Digital Valuation and Exam Grading

DigiVal is the next-generation digital valuation system, transforming traditional paper based grading into a fast, accurate, and fully digital process.

Paper Based grading is slow, inconsistent, and resource-intensive. DigiVal digitizes and automates the evaluation process, eliminating administrative burdens while ensuring precision, transparency, and security.

Key Features:
  • Efficient Digital Grading – Eliminates manual paper handling and reduces grading time significantly
  • Instant, Transparent Results – Fast-tracked evaluation process with real-time access to results
  • Bias-Free, Anonymous Marking – Ensures fair and standardized assessment across all subjects
  • Secure Digital Records – Prevents loss of answer sheets with cloud-based storage and access
  • Seamless Integration – Compatible with existing school management platforms

DigiVal modernizes the grading process, bringing speed, accuracy, and reliability to academic assessments.
